## ✈️  Supporting Scientific Leadership: Dr. Kynon J Benjamin Receives 2025 ACNP Travel Award

We are proud to share that **Dr. Kynon J Benjamin** has been awarded the
**2025 Travel Award** by the **American College of Neuropsychopharmacology (ACNP)**
— a prestigious honor recognizing outstanding early-career scientists in
neuropsychiatric research.

This award supports participation in the ACNP Annual Meeting, where rising
researchers engage with leaders in neuroscience, psychiatry, and pharmacology.

Dr. Benjamin’s selection reflects his growing impact in computational psychiatry
and commitment to transforming mental health research through innovative,
data-driven approaches.

We look forward to sharing new insights and building collaborations at this
year’s meeting!
